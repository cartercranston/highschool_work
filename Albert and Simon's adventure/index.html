Borg's Adventure


Use the arrow keys to move your sphere around.
You can't go through asteroid belts, forcefields or planets.
If you get hit by a ship or photon torpedo, you lose.
To win a level, you must first get adjacent to the planet.
Your drones will beam down to the planet, and after six seconds you will be allowed to return to the planet.
Finally, you will have to return to the cube.
Forcefields disappear when you beam your drones up from the planet.
